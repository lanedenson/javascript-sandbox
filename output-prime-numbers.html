Purpose:

To find all prime numbers (numIterate) between 0 and num. Prime numbers are those that are only divisible by 1 and themselves.

Approach:

Loop 1: Iterate through all integers less than or equal to num.

Loop 2: For each number, iterate through 2 and 1 less than that number (isItPrime)

Test to see if the humber is divisible by any number other than itself

Do this by using the remainder operator (%); divide numIterate by isItPrime. If the remainder is 0 at any point, then it is NOT prime
